• A Mano: Three-Course Menu

  • First Course Antipasti

    Classic Bruschetta with Homemade Focaccia A celebration of peak freshness. We start with our signature focaccia, baked in-house daily, sliced and lightly toasted. Topped with a generous serving of sun-ripened tomatoes, fresh basil, garlic, and a drizzle of premium extra-virgin olive oil. Simple, fragrant, and vibrant.

    Main Course (Secondi)

    Chicken Parmesan, Hand-Pounded A hearty, comforting classic. We hand-pound fresh chicken breasts to perfect tenderness, lightly bread them with our own herbed breadcrumbs, and fry until golden. Topped with rich, 6-hour simmered marinara sauce and fresh mozzarella, then baked to perfection and served alongside our scratch-made spaghetti.

    Dessert (Dolci)

    Nonna Elara's Tiramisu The culmination of our family's tradition. Layers of delicate ladyfingers are soaked in a rich espresso blend and layered with a cloud-like mascarpone cream. Dusted generously with imported cocoa powder, this is a true taste of Italian comfort.
